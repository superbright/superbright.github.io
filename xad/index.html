Instructions

Once you press download and choose install, go to your homescreen to find Vision app.

When app opened, you will see the message 'UNTRUSTED ENTERPRISE DEVELOPER'. Follow these steps:
  • Go to Settings > General > Device Management
  • Tap 'Primitives, Inc.'
  • Tap "Trust Primitives, Inc.'"
  • Open Application from Home Screen. Allow 'Vision' to Access the Camera

    Before the Fashion Show Experience, open the Vision application and hold up your phone to the stage like you're taking a video. Enjoy the augmented reality experience!
